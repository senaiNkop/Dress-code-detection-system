A Final Year Project

This project aims to develop a clothing monitoring system that will help students dress appropriately in accordance with their institution's dress code. This software will be an essential tool in ensuring that students dress appropriately and maintain high standards of decency in their attire. The system will be designed to provide feedback to students on their clothing choice, ensuring they dress within the boundaries of what is acceptable in their institution.

As a final year project, this research is expected to contribute to the existing body of knowledge on computer vision and deep learning. The development of this system requires a significant amount of data collection, image annotation, and training of the machine learning model, which may be a challenging task for an individual student. Therefore, the assistance of the public is highly appreciated in collecting images and annotating them for training purposes.

Moreover, the development of this system requires financial resources to cover the cost of acquiring the necessary hardware, software, and other resources required for the project. Any form of financial assistance is highly welcomed to facilitate the development of this innovative system.

This project aims to promote student compliance with dress codes, and this will ultimately lead to a positive impact on the educational environment. The successful completion of this project will provide a practical solution to an existing problem, thus making life easier for both students and educators alike.

We are confident that with the support of the community, we will be able to achieve our goals and make a significant impact on the lives of students in educational institutions. We thank you in advance for your support and look forward to working together towards a better future.
