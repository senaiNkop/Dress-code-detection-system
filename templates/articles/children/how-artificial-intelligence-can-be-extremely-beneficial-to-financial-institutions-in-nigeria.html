Background

#} {#
#}

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become immanent within every sector and is fundamentally and incrementally changing the world we live in for the better. In the world of banking, there are many areas where AI can be of enormous benefits. But it seems even with the rush of every major corporation in the adoption and development of AI technologies into their system, there has been sluggishness in the efforts by Nigerian corporations to join the rush in incorporating AI technologies into their companies even when they stand to benefit from it, most especially in the banking industries.

In a country where long queues at banking premises are the order of the day and extremely poor customer services, you would stand to reasons they would grab this opportunity with open arms and fervent implementation. I remember going to a bank for the simple objective of requesting a new ATM card but having to wait for over 5 hours before being able to make my request because of the large crowd of people waiting to have their request met, with only three overworked and snappish clerks having to meet their demands. Needless to say, I left there also feeling irritable, and this is a feeling a lot of Nigerians are familiar with.

Also, the news is filled with fraudulent activities committed by individuals ranging from illegal transactions to identity theft. These are problems that AI can easily solve and are brutally successful at it too!

When it comes to fraud detection, AI follows the paradigm “Since fraudsters I have learned to fly without perching, I have learned to shoot without missing”, meaning that AI will be able to match any fraudulent activities no matter how venturesome.

But lets look at the way AI can help alleviate the most important problems we face in the banking industry in Nigeria:

1. Compliance Detection

Banking is one of the most regulated sectors of the economy. Banking compliance means observing rules and regulations set in place and they are set in place to detect and address any, and all deviations from the company’s operations and all banks, as of 2020 there are over 130 acts and banking regulations that affect the banking industry in Nigeria.

To ensure compliance and reduce the risk of monetary fines, criminal charges and operational shutdowns, banks have to manually check to ensure all operations are safe, but these are always error-prone. To reduce this risk AI can help drastically reduce this error that humans often make when manually completing them by automating the process and providing due diligence in the process. Intelligent systems trained in such a way ensure new regulations can be intercepted faster and more accurately. This allows banks to maintain accurate records regarding any customer at any given time.

2. Reduced Costs

AI software can perform labour extensive and sensitive tasks without any error thereby helping financial institution’s agents avoid making different errors due to the high volume of interactions with customers in a day, improving the overall efficiency of the company. Human errors are so common that research shows that 38% of banking losses are due to human error. AI software reduces the demand for less-skilled labour and improves the efficiency of the remaining bank staff, as employee benefits consistently represent the lion share of the bank cost base.

3. Customer Service

I have yet to walk into a bank premise and not see the customer service desks surrounded by disgruntled customers demanding solutions to their grievances with the customer service provider getting more disagreeable as the day goes on, on top of that customers expect banks to provide customer services 24/7.

AI Virtual Assistant (ChatBot) is one of the primary benefits of AI in the banking industry. AI-powered chatbots ensure that customers can get answers to their questions quickly and creating an individualized experience for the customers. It frees the employees to perform more complex tasks and satisfy customers by answering the question asked to solve a problem.

Chatbots simulates a 24/7 customer service making the customer feel satisfied that they request are readily answered by an agent. I long for the day when I don’t have to visit any bank to have resolutions to my every little request.

4. Fraud Detection

This is where AI truly shines and where it can soundly outperform humans, it is implemented to combat fraud and money laundering. It can also be used to spot inconsistencies, without AI banks loses millions because of criminal activities. It does this by analyzing large amounts of data and identifying suspicious transactions, with such information, the bank can review and determine if it’s a fraudulent payment, all of which can be performed in real-time.

What does this mean in a nutshell? Suppose I as a customer have been making purchases consisting mainly of phones and phones accessories online, but if purchase for a car is then charged to my account. AI would see this purchase as inconsistency with my purchase history and price ranges and immediately alarm the bank authorities to the possibility of a fraudulent activity about to take place while at the same time preventing the purchase from going through (all this happening without the customer even been aware of it) and then finally notifying the customer of the attempt.

Conclusion

AI can generate customer insights that can be used for personalized communication, offers and services. Also, the banking sector has been at the forefront of the adoption of new technologies. In the United State alone billions of dollars have been invested in the research and development of AI. The integration of AI in the financial sector is predicted to lower costs by 20% while still boosting productivity. Nigeria Banking sectors should endeavour to take advantage of this amazing technology now that it is at the infancy stage thereby reaping maximum benefit from it as it grows into maturity.
